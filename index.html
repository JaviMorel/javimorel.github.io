Since that, I have been learning and creating games in groups or alone to prove what can I do. In this portfolio there are some examples of games which I have done along this time.

Skills

I have experience in creating games on Unity and Unreal Engine 4, using C# and C++ as the programming language. Furthermore, I made a course by Game Dev called "Unreal Multiplayer Master: Video Game Dev In C++ Course"

Final Grade Project-Genetic Algorithm for making sports schedules in Round Robin tournaments

As the final grade project, I did an investigation on genetic algorithms and I created one which generates sports schedules in round robin tournaments (like a football league system) with restrictions. Moreover, I made an application on Unity (with C#) to show an usage example of this algorithm.

Game Projects

Since I started learning programming, I have participated in some game projects which had been uploaded to Itch.io. Some of them, I made them alone but, however, I have created others in groups.

Trading Paradise

Trading Paradise is a puzzle game. You start with an item and your goal is, by trading, obtain the goal item. This game has two modes, one player and two players. On "One player mode", you have a time to finish the game or you will lose, and on the other side, on "Two players mode", you can play with a friend and compite to see who is faster and takes the goal item first.

I made this game alone, including the art part, the design of the game and the programming part.

Daedalus Stargazer

Space (and very special) game in which you control very particular beings (they are made of particles) and you must collect the Lost Stars. This game was made in Unreal Engine for GlobalGameJam2021.

I participated in this game jam with a team and I was responsible for making the mechanics of the game, the movement of the player, the game functionality, etc.

Fujiori

It's been centuries since Opako Shimura froze on the top of mt. Fuji! You have to help him to climb down and fulfill an old promise.This game was made in Unreal Engine for EpicMegaJam2020.

In this game jam, I have also participated with a team and I was the gameplay programmer. I made the movement of one of the game modes, the lose and win mechanisms, etc.

Sweet Alliance

Sweet Alliance is a local multiplayer cooperative game where you have to protect the Cake from the hungry sweets. However, it will not be easy because each player controls the movement of their wagon and the cannon of the other. This game was made for Global StartupCities GameJam.

I made this game with a team and I was responsible for the enemies spawn, the lose mechanisms and some powerups actions.

Switch On

Switch On is a game where you control Joe, a pacient who has woken up in a strange clinic being color blind and you will have to restore his sight and get out of there without being seen. This game was developed for the Futuristic Jam 2020.

This was the first Game Jam which I had participated and I did it with a team. I was responsible for the enemies' behaviour, the streaming levels and the mechanism of the doors.

War bot

War bot is a game to create a war between the players (max 30 names) and this game was created to recreate the famous Twitter bots that were created in 2018 which did the same thing.

I made this game alone and it was made for PC and Android mobiles.

The hunting of the bones

You are a dog called "Choco" and your purpose is to take all the bones around the house without being seen by the cats.

I made this game for a subject in my degree of Computer Engineering and I made the whole game alone, including the 3D models and animations.
